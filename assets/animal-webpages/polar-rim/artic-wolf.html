ARTIC WOLF

  • Often called the "polar wolf" or "white wolf," Arctic wolves inhabit the Arctic regions of North America and Greenland.

  • Wolfs understand that a one man army is not always enough therefore when they go out hunting they often do travel in packs consiting of 2 or 15 wolf individuals. However they could increase to more than 30 members.

  • Arctic wolves primarily prey on muskoxen and Arctic hares. They have also been found to prey on lemmings, caribou, Arctic foxes, birds, and beetles

  • If the wolves go hunting one adult normally stays behind to take care of the little ones.
