About Us

Our Story

We are more than just a zoo; we are a sanctuary for both animals and humans alike. Our mission is to foster understanding and respect for wildlife while promoting conservation efforts globally. With a team dedicated to animal welfare and education, we strive to create meaningful experiences for visitors of all ages.

Why Should You Care?

We believe that education is key to fostering empathy and respect for all living beings. That's why we go beyond traditional zoo experiences to train regular civilians on how to interact with animals. From learning how to hug and kiss to commanding even the most shy or aggressive animals, we empower visitors to overcome fear and forge connections.

Our Objectives

  • Our Mission

    To promote conservation, education, and empathy through meaningful interactions with wildlife.

  • Our Vision

    Compassion, respect, sustainability, and integrity guide everything we do.

  • Our Training

    Our journey to enable communication between humans and animals has been arduous. We've invested in extensive research and training for both employees and animals to facilitate understanding and cooperation.
